The Veiled Compass Coalition operates from the belief that true authority must remain unseen to remain objective. Based in the subterranean archives of Old Town, they dedicate their lives to the preservation and interpretation of the "Cartographer's Records," a collection of maps and prophecies dating back to the city's founding.

While the Silver Ledger Syndicate handles the city's overt economy, the Coalition works in the silence between the numbers. They function as a clandestine advisory board, offering "guidance" to Old Town's ruling officials. This guidance is rarely direct; instead, it is delivered through cryptic letters—often accompanied by a simple, silver-inlaid compass—that nudge the city's leaders toward decisions that align with the long-term preservation of the archives.

Rumors persist that the Coalition is not merely preserving history, but actively shaping it, carefully pruning the branches of the city's future to ensure that Old Town survives whatever calamities the Cartographer predicted. Membership is anonymous; to join is to relinquish one's public identity entirely, becoming nothing more than a nameless hand guiding the needle of the city's destiny.
