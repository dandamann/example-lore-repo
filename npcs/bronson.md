Bronson, known to the locals as Big Bron, is a four-foot-six, rotund dwarf whose reputation in Old Town far exceeds his stature. While his physical presence is often described as imposing, he is a man of few words and mysterious habits. 

He serves as a quiet anchor for the Cool Guys faction. He isn't one to boast about his past, and there are whispers that his rounded appearance hides a surprisingly agile fighter who has spent decades navigating the back alleys and underworld politics of the city. He maintains a stoic exterior, acting as a watchful guardian for his associates, though what drove him to leave his ancestral mountain halls for the cramped, stone-slicked streets of Old Town remains a secret he keeps tucked tightly away.
